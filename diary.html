    BRADLEY SEYMOUR

    CREATIVE & ART DIRECTOR

    Inspired by the axiom beauty equals truth, Australian art director Bradley Seymour strives for authentic communication via his natural curiosity and ability to listen effectively. As former creative director at Marie Claire Italia, he consistently delivers a vibrant, authoritative point of difference. Meticulously fine-tuning the balance between print and digital, Seymour continues to launch new titles and re-define the new print media.

     

    His editorial experience in fashion and his penchant for interior and product design have inspired Seymour to embark on a new creative journey: Studio-Sibling.com. Seymour's Milan-based agency focuses his international point of view on the intersection between fashion and design.
